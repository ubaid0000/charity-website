About Kind Heart

We are a charity organization driven by compassion and powered by people who want to make the world a better place for children and families in need.

Our Mission

To provide education, healthcare, and hope to underprivileged communities across the country.

Our Vision

A world where every child has a chance to grow, learn, and thrive — regardless of background.

Our Values

Compassion, Transparency, Dedication, Equality, and Empowerment.

Our Journey

  • 2018: Founded by a group of university volunteers
  • 2019: Opened our first school support center
  • 2021: Helped 10,000+ children with healthcare and food
  • 2023: Expanded to 3 more cities
